What is the event

BAPC

  • Programming competition
  • Solve algorithmic problems in five hours
  • 3 students, 1 computer
  • A lot more paper involved; printing code/problem set etc.

Target group

  • Check the ICPC Regionals Eligibility Decision Tree
  • Basicly maximum first year master
  • In general all institutes (uni/hbo) of the BeNeLux:
    1. Belgium
    2. Netherlands (most teams)
    3. Luxembourg (not represented yet)

Selection

  • Jury consists of people from the field/universities
  • Optional to have preliminaries at your institute:
    1. More teams per university than BAPC spots
    2. Practice round for BAPC
    3. For the VU, it's AAPP
  • Up to the university which team to promote
  • After BAPC -> NWERC -> WF

Onsite

  • Saturday event, from 9.00 till ~20.00
  • Many volunteers needed
  • Registration desk! Welcoming teams to the VU!
  • Helping sponsors, tech & AV
  • Runners needed to help during the contest
  • For example, handing out printed code, security etc.
  • Prep: making goodiebags, setup contest floor, the network drinks etc.
  • Post: teardown

Online

  • Use a geeky platform
  • Send goodiebags
  • High class booklet
  • Stream jury presentation
  • 3 persons, 3 computers format
  • Hard to get participant sponsor interaction
  • Improve on the Delft standard (last year)
  • Expected money will be enough (!?)
